About Us!

Welcome to KP Sarelli's, where Italian tradition meets family warmth since 2007! Nestled in the heart of Bayonne, New Jersey, KP Sarelli's is a beloved family-owned restaurant that brings the authentic flavors of Italy to your table.

From classic pasta dishes to mouthwatering pizzas, every bite at KP Sarelli's is crafted with love and tradition. Our menu features a delightful array of Italian favorites, made using the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're craving a cozy dinner with loved ones or celebrating a special occasion, our warm ambiance and attentive service ensure that every visit is memorable.

At KP Sarelli's, we pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where guests feel like part of the family. Come experience the taste of Italy, right here in Bayonne! Join us at KP Sarelli's for an unforgettable dining experience that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Italy!
