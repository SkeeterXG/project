About Us!

Welcome to Kp Sarelli's, a culinary haven nestled in the heart of Bayonne, NJ, where the flavors of Northern Italy come alive in a cozy, inviting atmosphere. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience where good food, great company, and live music on the weekends converge to create magical moments.

At Kp Sarelli's, we're passionate about bringing the authentic taste of Northern Italian cuisine to our guests. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted by the tantalizing aromas of freshly prepared dishes, the sounds of live music, and the promise of the best bread and butter you'll ever taste.

Indulge your senses with our signature Chicken Marsala in Mushroom Sauce, a dish that continues to captivate the hearts and palates of our patrons. Picture succulent chicken breast bathed in a luscious mushroom sauce infused with the rich flavors of Marsala wine, each bite a symphony of savory bliss.

And when it comes to desserts, prepare to be enchanted by our Strawberry Zabaglione. Imagine plump, juicy strawberries delicately enveloped in a velvety zabaglione sauce, their sweetness elevated to new heights with every spoonful.

Or perhaps you prefer something indulgently creamy? Look no further than our Ricotta Cheesecake, a decadent delight that melts in your mouth with every forkful. Made with creamy ricotta cheese and a hint of citrus, it's a heavenly finale to your Northern Italian feast.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, catching up with friends, or simply seeking a moment of culinary escapism, Kp Sarelli's promises an experience like no other. Soak in the vibrant atmosphere, savor the exquisite flavors, and let the live music serenade you into a state of gastronomic bliss.

Join us at Kp Sarelli's in Bayonne, NJ, and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the timeless traditions and vibrant flavors of Northern Italy. Buon appetito!
